Privacy Policy for DocOpen

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Wick Labs ("we", "our") operates the DocOpen mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect

DocOpen is designed to work fully offline and does not require an account. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your documents or personal files.

The App may collect the following limited data:

  • Advertising Identifiers: The App displays banner ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID and basic device information for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Purchase Information: If you purchase the ad-removal in-app product, the transaction is processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Document contents — your files never leave your device
  • Account information — no sign-up required
  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics

Permissions

The App requests storage access only to read documents you choose to open. No files are uploaded or shared.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — for displaying banner ads. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — for processing the ad-removal purchase. Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
